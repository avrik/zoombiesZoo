1 Match & Trap

  • match 3 stones , get 1 brick
  • match 3 bricks - can collect
  • match 3 trees , get 1 lumber
  • match 3 lumber - can collect
  • trap 3 people/animal , get 1 tomb
  • match 3 tombs, get 1 pile of bones
  • match 3 pile of bones , get 1 oil reservoir
  • match 3 oil reservoir - get 1 oil barrel

2Collect

  • more than 3 bricks on a tile, can be collected to the city storage
  • more than 3 lumber on a tile, can be collected to the city sawmill

3Build ,Populate & Grow

  • use collected resources (bricks, lumber and coins) to develop your city
  • build roads to lead the wondering people to their houses
  • build regular houses to the peasants
  • build churchs to populate holy men
  • build palaces to populate the royal
  • match 3 same type building to upgrade it

6Expand

expand your city, buy more land!
